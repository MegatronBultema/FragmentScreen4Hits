FraggleRockHit

Machine Learning Applied to Fragment Based Virtual Screening
Developed by Megan Bultema (Carter)

FraggleRockHit is a virtual screening approach for drug development. Machine learning algorithms trained using fragment libaries are used to predict hit propensity of traditional high-throughput screening libaries. The application of a FraggleRock model, unique to your target, can help you reduce the size of your potentail screening library-- saving time and resources in your drug discovery process. {% endblock %}
